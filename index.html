Who We Are

About InAmigos Foundation

InAmigos Foundation is a Section 8 registered non-profit organization founded in 2020. The organization works across India to create sustainable social impact through education, women empowerment, animal welfare, environmental conservation, community service, and skill development initiatives.

Why Volunteer With Us?

Make an active contribution to society while developing your own skills and joining a vast volunteer community.

Meaningful Social Impact

Be the catalyst for real change. Direct your energy to programs that feed, educate, shelter, and green our society directly.

Skill Development

Learn valuable hands-on abilities in operations, digital literacy, project management, and community outreach.

Community Engagement

Join a network of over 200+ volunteers and 30,000+ past interns. Collaborate, grow, and build lasting friendships.

Leadership Opportunities

Take charge of city-level projects, mentor fresh recruits, and organize regional distribution campaigns.
